My Colombia Travel Bucket List

Destination: Pamplona, Norte de Santander

My Travel Experience

If I visited Pamplona, I would explore its colonial streets and admire its historic churches. Visitors can learn about history and should enjoy the cool weather.

Where to Go

  • Main Square
  • Historic Churches
  • Local Museums

What to Do

  • Take photos
  • Try traditional food
  • Walk around the historic center

Travel Tips

You should bring warm clothes because the weather can be cold. You must respect historical places.
