My Colombia Travel Bucket List

Pamplona, Norte de Santander

My Travel Experience

If I visited Pamplona, I would explore its colonial streets. Visitors can learn about history and should enjoy the cool weather.

Where to Go

You can visit the main square, historic churches, and local museums.

What to Do

You should take photos, try traditional food, and walk around the historic center.

Travel Tips

You must bring warm clothes and respect historical places.
